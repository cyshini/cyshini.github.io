Méert

  • Art Direction
  • UI Design

Give new strength and coherence to the online presence and universe of the famous pastry shop from Lille.

At the time of the redesign of meert.fr, the brand had no fewer than 4 sites: 2 online stores and 2 showcase websites. The objective was therefore to strengthen and centralize everything on a single platform, supported by a single, more ambitious artistic direction, evocative and respectful of their centuries-old history and know-how.
